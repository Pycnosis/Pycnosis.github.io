OUR MISSION

Our mission at ResQ revolves around rescuing and redistributing surplus, perfectly edible food, aiming to establish an efficient and sustainable system that transforms excess resources into a solution for those in need. We envision a society free from hunger, where mindful eating habits contribute to the health of our planet. By using cutting-edge technologies and smart partnerships, we connect excess food from cafes, restaurants, and charitable organizations through creative food recovery networks. The damaging effects of food waste on the environment, the economy, and society are something we are dedicated to spreading awareness of. Our team's commitment lies in bringing about constructive transformation, supporting laws that facilitate the redistribution of food, and continuously improving our procedures to optimize our influence. With a vision of a world where ethical food practices lead to a healthy planet, join us at ResQ as we fight diligently to battle hunger and food waste, developing sustainable systems via innovation and partnership. As we travel together toward a future with greater sustainability.

About Us

ResQ, named to reflect the company's central mission centered around rescue, is at the forefront of creative ways to combat food waste. The company promotes this cause by developing cutting-edge apps and solutions. ResQ's major goal is to rescue surplus food, preventing it from going to waste and positively impacting the environment. ResQ reflects the company's persistent commitment to reducing food waste, which includes developing practical and effective methods to solve this crucial issue. The company focuses on using technology to effectively rescue food, leading to a more sustainable and responsible food ecology.

About ResQFy

ResQFy is a cutting-edge mobile application that connects surplus food from cafes, restaurants, and charitable organizations through creative food recovery networks. Our mission is to make the process of rescuing surplus food as simple and convenient as possible for our users. ResQFy is a platform that fosters a sense of community and camaraderie among food lovers, allowing them to find each other and share their experiences.

OUR VISION

We envision “ResQ” to be a respected and adaptable company known worldwide as an innovator in providing an innovative application that saves and reduces food surplus loss as an environmental solution to food waste and food insecurity.
